# PathologyNet

Comprehensive pathology sample tracking and diagnosis management system ensuring quality control and expert collaboration in histopathological analysis.

## Overview

PathologyNet provides end-to-end pathology workflow management from sample collection to final diagnosis, including second opinion consultations, quality assurance, and molecular testing integration.

## Features

- **Sample Tracking**: Complete chain of custody from collection to diagnosis
- **Pathologist Network**: Certified pathologist registration and caseload management
- **Diagnosis Management**: Structured diagnostic reporting with confidence scoring
- **Second Opinions**: Expert consultation system for complex or uncertain cases
- **Quality Assurance**: Comprehensive QA reviews with corrective action tracking
- **Molecular Integration**: Molecular testing coordination and result integration

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions

- `register-pathologist`: Register certified pathologists with subspecialty credentials
- `submit-pathology-sample`: Submit tissue samples with preservation and custody details
- `update-sample-quality`: Assess and update sample quality status during processing
- `submit-diagnosis`: Provide comprehensive pathological diagnosis with findings
- `request-second-opinion`: Request expert consultation for complex cases
- `provide-consultation`: Deliver second opinion consultation with recommendations
- `conduct-quality-assurance`: Perform QA review with quality metrics assessment
- `add-molecular-test`: Integrate molecular testing results with pathology findings

### Read-Only Functions

- `get-pathology-sample`: Retrieve complete sample information and chain of custody
- `get-pathology-diagnosis`: Access detailed diagnostic findings and interpretations
- `get-pathologist-profile`: View pathologist credentials and current caseload
- `get-second-opinion`: Review consultation details and expert recommendations
- `get-quality-assurance`: Check QA review results and corrective actions
- `get-molecular-test`: Access molecular testing results and clinical significance
- `get-next-sample-id`: Get the next available sample identifier
- `get-next-opinion-id`: Get the next available opinion identifier
- `get-next-test-id`: Get the next available test identifier

## Usage

Deploy the contract and register certified pathologists to begin comprehensive pathology workflow management with quality assurance and expert consultation capabilities.
